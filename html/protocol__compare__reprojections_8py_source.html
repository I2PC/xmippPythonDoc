55 class XmippProtCompareReprojections(ProtAnalysis3D, ProjMatcher):
56  """Compares a set of classes or averages with the corresponding projections of a reference volume.
57  The set of images must have a 3D angular assignment and the protocol computes the residues
58  (the difference between the experimental images and the reprojections). The zscore of the mean
59  and variance of the residues are computed. Large values of these scores may indicate outliers.
60  The protocol also analyze the covariance matrix of the residual and computes the logarithm of
61  its determinant [Cherian2013]. The extremes of this score (called zScoreResCov), that is
62  values particularly low or high, may indicate outliers."""
63

65  _lastUpdateVersion = VERSION_3_0
66  _devStatus = UPDATED
67  _possibleOutputs = {}
